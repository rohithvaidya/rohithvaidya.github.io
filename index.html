Hey !

My major in computer science/ algorithms with couple of product internships and an entrepreneurial bug bite taught me that novel, inclusive engineering designs in conjunction with digital fabrication can lead to innovative products and also help build sustainable structures.

As we move into the next digital industrial revolution, design softwares must act as a companion for designers given the high computational power we have. I am excited to be researching the latest advances in the field which will help build the next generation of design softwares to obtain novel product designs.

It also turns out that nature, which is slowly disappearing, has some tough lessons in store. Thus, my research lies at the intersection of computational geometry/ mechanics, bio-morphologies and structural design.
